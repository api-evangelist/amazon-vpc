AWS introduces fine-grained IAM permissions for Route 53 Profiles using new condition keys, enabling each team to receive only the permissions they need to reduce risk and increase operational efficiency. The post provides three real-world scenarios for scoping permissions across application, security, and network teams managing DNS at scale.

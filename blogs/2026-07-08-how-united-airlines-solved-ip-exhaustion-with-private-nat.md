United Airlines, a major U.S. airline headquartered in Chicago, Illinois, moves over 181 million passengers annually across a global route network. When severe weather grounds flights or air traffic control issues cascade across the network, United Airlines needs every system to scale instantly, rebooking passengers, reassigning crews, reprocessing flight data.
